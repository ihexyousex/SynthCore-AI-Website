What is Synthetic Data?

And why does every AI company need it in 2026

❓ The Problem

Training AI models requires massive datasets. But real data is expensive (manual labeling costs $5-20 per record), slow (months to collect), and dangerous (privacy violations, PII exposure, GDPR fines).


💡 The Solution

Synthetic data is AI-generated training data that mirrors the statistical patterns of real data — without containing any real personal information. It's the "Digital Twin" of your dataset: structurally identical, legally safe, and infinitely scalable. Gartner predicts that by 2026, 60% of data used for AI will be synthetic.


🏭 What SynthCore AI Does

SynthCore AI is a self-hosted engine that reads your documents (PDFs, Markdown, code, websites) and autonomously generates thousands of high-quality Q&A pairs, reasoning chains, and instruction-following records — ready for fine-tuning GPT, Llama, Mistral, or any LLM.
